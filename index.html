Grand Theft Auto VI is an upcoming action-adventure game in development by Rockstar Games. It is due to be the eighth main Grand Theft Auto game, following Grand Theft Auto V 2013, and the sixteenth entry overall. Set within the fictional open world state of Leonida based on Florida and its Miami-inspired Vice City, the story is expected to follow criminal duo Lucia and her male partner
Following years of speculation and anticipation, Rockstar confirmed in February 2022 that the game was in development. The game was formally revealed in December 2023 and is scheduled to be released in 2025 for the PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X/S.
